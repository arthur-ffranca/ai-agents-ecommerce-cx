Architecture workflow

AI support that checks policy before acting.

01

Customer Message

The system receives a support request such as refund, return, exchange, order tracking or escalation.

02

Intent Router

Classifies the customer intent and detects the required workflow.

03

Policy Check / RAG

Retrieves the relevant company policy and validates if the action is allowed.

04

Workflow Planner

Chooses the safest next step based on order status, refund limit, return window and customer context.

05

Tool Execution

Calls the correct operational system, such as Shopify, CRM, order database or support queue.

06

Final Response / Escalation

Generates a customer-ready answer with the approved action, denial reason or human escalation.
